About HashHero

HashHero is a lightweight, free stop for content creators. Generate smart hashtags, ask AI for quick strategy tips, create social QR codes, and keep momentum with a focused engagement timer — all in one place.

Our goal is to remove friction so you can publish faster, learn what works, and grow your audience. No paywall, no sign‑up — just practical tools that respect your time.

  • Smart Hashtag Generator — context‑aware suggestions with popularity tiers and impact estimates.
  • Ask AI — instant answers for captions, hooks, and growth tactics.
  • QR Codes — quick links to your socials without tracking.
  • Engagement Timer — sprint in focused work/break cycles.
