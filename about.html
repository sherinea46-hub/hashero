About HashHero

HashHero is a free, fast, no-login toolkit for creators. The hashtag generator is the star; everything else is an add-on that helps you ship content faster.

  • Generator: Bias-aware tags from your description with per-tag copy.
  • Meme: Upload or pick a template, add draggable text, export PNG/JPG.
  • Ask AI: Quick prompts for hooks, titles, and captions.
  • QR: Simple QR codes for links.
  • Timer: Engagement countdowns with reset.
  • Audio: Hidden-until-search previews powered by iTunes (30s).
