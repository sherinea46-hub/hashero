How we calculate

Weighted engagement = Likes × L + Comments × C + Shares × S + Saves × Save. ER by Followers = Weighted ÷ Followers × 100. ER by Views = Weighted ÷ Views × 100. Presets are simple multipliers to reflect relative effort (comments/shares/saves are often stronger signals). This is a guidance tool — platforms use proprietary formulas.
